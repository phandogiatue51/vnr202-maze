# 🏆 Maze Quest: Heritage Journey - Detailed Rules

## 1. Objective
Players take on the role of researchers/soldiers navigating a complex maze to collect **heritage documents**. The winner is the player who collects the **most documents** AND successfully **reaches the exit**.
- If multiple players reach the exit, the one with the most documents wins.
- If time runs out before anyone reaches the exit, the player with the most documents wins.

## 2. Movement
- Use **Arrow keys** (↑ ↓ ← →) or **WASD** keys (W A S D) to navigate the maze.
- You cannot pass through walls. The system automatically blocks invalid moves.
- Mobile users can use the on-screen joystick.

## 3. Collecting Documents & Challenges
- There are multiple documents placed at fixed locations throughout the maze.
- **When you touch a document:**
    - The game pauses for you and displays a **Multiple Choice Question (MCQ)**.
    - You must select the correct answer to claim the document.
- **Outcomes:**
    - **Correct Answer:** You receive the document (+1 point).
    - **Incorrect Answer:** You don't get the document, and it remains in place for others to try.
- **Note:** Each document can only be collected **once**. The first person to answer correctly claims it for their score.

## 4. Questions
- Each document has its own unique question.
- Format: 4 options (A, B, C, D).
- There is no time limit for answering, but remember that other players are still moving and collecting other documents while you are stopped!

## 5. Reaching the Finish Line
- The exit is located at a specific corner of the maze.
- **Requirement:** You must have collected **at least 1 document** to be allowed to finish. You cannot enter the exit with zero documents.
- Once you reach the exit:
    - Your final document count is locked.
    - You can no longer move.
    - Your status on the leaderboard changes to **"Finished"**.

## 6. Time & Game End
- Each match lasts **10 minutes** (countdown timer is visible on screen).
- When time hits 0:00:
    - All movement stops.
    - The player with the highest document count wins.
    - If scores are tied, the player who finished earlier takes priority.

## 7. Leaderboard
- A real-time leaderboard displays:
    - Current document count for every player.
    - Status: "Racing" or "Finished."
    - Real-time ranking based on scores.

## 8. Items & Power-ups
You can find and use special items throughout the maze:

| Item | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 🔦 Torch | Shows path to exit for 10 seconds |
| 🛡️ Shield | Blocks 1 negative effect |
| 💣 Bomb | Makes target opponent lose 1 document |
| 🕸️ Net | Immobilizes target for 5 seconds |
| ✨ Flash | Blinds target for 2 seconds |
| 💨 Smoke | Obscures target's vision for 5 seconds |
| 🍌 Banana | Trap - reverses controls for 10 seconds |
